ITAR Visitor Briefing — Sunset Industries

Welcome. Sunset Industries manufactures components for U.S. defense customers. Some of the work in this facility is governed by the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR), 22 CFR Parts 120–130. This briefing covers what that means for your visit.

What ITAR Means For You

  • Defense-related drawings and parts are protected. If you see something marked ITAR, military, or with an export-control notice, that's controlled information.
  • "Deemed export" rules apply on-site. Showing a drawing, screen, or part to a person who is not a U.S. person is treated as an export under U.S. federal law.
  • You are here because Sunset has confirmed you qualify as a U.S. person under § 120.62.

While You're Here, You Must NOT

  • Take photos, videos, audio recordings, or screen captures of any work, drawings, parts, or screens.
  • Use a USB drive, external hard drive, or any removable media on Sunset's systems.
  • Discuss specific Sunset work, drawings, programs, or customers with anyone outside Sunset.
  • Enter areas you weren't authorized for. If you're not sure where you can go, ask your point of contact.
  • Email yourself or anyone else any document, drawing, photo, or technical information from Sunset.

What You SHOULD Do

  • Place your phone in the designated phone storage area near the lockers/bathrooms while on the floor.
  • If you need to take notes for your own work, keep them general — no part numbers, specifications, or program names.
  • If you see something that looks like a compliance issue, tell Alex Hauptman, Peter Hauptman, or Karen Hauptman.

Consequences of Violation

Unauthorized release of ITAR-controlled technical data carries civil penalties up to $1.27M per violation and potential criminal penalties under 22 CFR §§ 127.3 and 127.10. These apply to individuals, not just companies.

Questions?

Talk to Alex Hauptman or Peter Hauptman (the Empowered Official) any time.

This briefing is part of Sunset's ITAR Compliance Policy v1.1 (May 2026), Sections 11, 12, and 13.
